## Preface

This document is an initial draft for the constitution of a future democratic Iran. The purpose of publishing this document is to initiate a public and participatory dialogue about the fundamental principles of the future government.

> **Note**: This document is a work in progress and everyone can submit their comments and suggestions through the "Suggest Amendment" button.

## Chapter One: General Principles

### Article 1
Iran is a republican and democratic country where sovereignty belongs to the people.

### Article 2
The inherent dignity, freedom, and equality of all human beings is the foundation of the country's legal and political system.

### Article 3
The separation of religion and state is guaranteed, and there is no official religion or ideology.

## Chapter Two: Fundamental Rights

### Article 4
Freedom of expression, freedom of the press, and freedom of access to information are inalienable rights of every citizen.

### Article 5
Freedom of peaceful assembly and association is guaranteed.
